TRAVELLING

I love the excitement of it all — feeling like an explorer — discovering a new land, a new language and all the little challenges that go along with it. When I travel, I am more spontaneous and don’t live for the future or the past… just in the moment. Being impulsive and saying ‘yes’ to nearly everything is part of the fun.
